Contact Us


I am always looking for prospective Ph.D. or M.S. students, interns and research assistants to work with.

Applicants should have a Bachelors or Masters degree in geophysics, geology, math, computer science, physics, engineering, or a closely related field. Applicants with prior experience in computer programming, remote sensing, volcanology or seismology are particularly encouraged to apply. To introduce yourself or ask about potential opportunities, please contact me at wenbin.xu@polyu.edu.hk. For info about applying to our graduate program please see http://www.lsgi.polyu.edu.hk/home/index.asp. If you are interested in applying for a PhD fellowship: http://www.polyu.edu.hk/ro/hkphd-fellowship/en/; https://cerg1.ugc.edu.hk/hkpfs/index.html

My research focuses on the use of remote sensing and radar interferometry (InSAR) to study ground deformation associated with a variety of geophysical and anthropogenic processes. Possible topics of research include using space geodetic data and remote sensing technique to study earthquakes, volcanoes, land subsidence, InSAR time series, atmospheric corrections, etc.

Please visit the our group webpage for details. In addition to working in my group at PolyU, you will also have the opportunity to interact with scientists at other organizations.
