Overview


Welcome to our website!

We are a research group in the department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics (LSGI) at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). Our research primarily focuses on the use of space geodetic data (e.g. InSAR and GPS) and land surveying techniques to study ground deformation associated with a variety of geophysical and anthropogenic processes. We are especially interested in the study of earthquake cycle deformation, volcanoes, land subsidence and atmosphere.



(The Earthquake Volcano and InSAR Group in Nov 2018: Sam Ho, Eslam Ali Saleh, Xiaoge Liu, Zeyu Chen, Xiong Zhao, Wing Lau, Sandra Yau, Wenbin Xu, Angie Leung, Lei Xie)
